Add Devices

Help Bot 2020

To find the hostname of your computer, simply open the command line and type 'hostname', then paste the result here in the hostname section and hit save. Done! Now your device has been added to your file sharing list and will now appear in your home page ready to be connected!

Connect to Device

Help Bot 2020

To connect to a device, look for the hostname of the device on your home page. Once you have found it click on the button. Then wait for a moment, A list of folders will show up, click on the one you would like to access, then click on the file and the file will open in your browser!

Remove Device

If you would no longer like to conenct to a device for any reasons and would like to delete it from your home page, go to this link, select the device and the on the bottom left you will find the delete option, just click it and confirm, Done! The device will no longer show up!
